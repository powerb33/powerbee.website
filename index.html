Product Strategy

As products are built, there is a constant tradeoff between speed and quality. In all cases the decisions that are made today affect the functionality of the future product. We help companies make the right decisions and tradeoffs today, to get to market quickly, while having the underlying architectural and product infrastructure for product growth.

🔍

Purpose & Fit

We start by understanding the product purpose, who are the intended users and groups and what functionality is needed for each. Every product decision made should satisfy functionality for the most important user groups. As we work to understand the product, we separate the true product requirements from existing solutions.

📋

Planning

Next we work on what the MVP will be and what follow-on functionality will be coming next. This is an important part in making sure that decisions made today don't limit future directions. If proof of concepts are required, they would happen here with user group check-ins to ensure each group's needs are being met.

🏗️

Architecture

In building the product architecture, we start with the data. Most databases are built to mirror services which makes growing products difficult as services change or are added regularly. By starting first with how the data should be organized and accessed, we help enable new services to be created or existing services to be changed much more quickly and easily.

📊

Tracking & Analytics

Being able to track your product features and use cases is crucial to prioritizing your solutions. We help companies setup a system that is easy to maintain and use, ensuring features are combined for easier development and nothing gets lost. We also help create analytical data that feeds your product roadmap and lifecycle.

💰

Pricing

We help companies look at different pricing strategies and incorporate these into the product architecture. We start with understanding the value that your product is worth for your customers, segmenting your users into possible tiers to help find the right pricing strategy for your company.
